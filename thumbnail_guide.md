# AI Course Thumbnail Generation Rules

This document outlines the visual and technical rules for generating thumbnails for AI-generated courses in the **GenCourse** ecosystem. These rules ensure that all generated content maintains the **Cyber-Gamified Dark Theme** and feels premium, cohesive, and high-fidelity.

---

## 🎨 Visual Style: "Cyber-Fantasy Fusion"

The thumbnails should feel like high-quality game assets—specifically, items or environments found in a futuristic RPG.

### 1. Style Keywords
- **Primary Style:** 3D Isometric, High-Gloss, Low-Poly (stylized), or Digital Illustration.
- **Lighting:** Cinematic, volumetric lighting with glowing neon edges.
- **Background:** Deep space or dark atmospheric voids (`#0b0c15`). Use "Atmospheric Glows" in the background that match the subject's accent color.

### 2. Core Color Palette
Generated images MUST adhere to the following accent colors based on the course category:

| Category | Accent Color | Hex Reference |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **General/Core** | Primary Purple | `#a855f7` |
| **Technical/Code** | Skill Cyan | `#38bdf8` |
| **Growth/AI** | Success Emerald | `#10b981` |
| **Economics/Rare** | Alert Amber | `#fbbf24` |
| **Secondary/Aux** | Magic Indigo | `#6366f1` |

---

## 📐 Composition & Framing

To maintain consistency in the "Explore Realms" and "Quests" sections, follow these framing rules:

- **Subject Placement:** Single, iconic subject centered in the frame.
- **Perspective:** 3D Isometric (3/4 view) or Front-facing.
- **Floating Effect:** The subject should appear to be floating or resting on a subtle, glowing platform/pedestal.
- **Depth of Field:** Soft blur on the background to make the central asset "pop."

---

## 🏛️ Subject Matter Guidelines

The imagery should represent the course topic using "Gamified Metaphors."

| Course Topic | Recommended Visual Asset |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Programming** | Glowing crystal shards, floating circuit-etched cubes, or holographic scrolls. |
| **History/Arts** | Ancient stone artifacts with neon runes, leather-bound books with glowing seals. |
| **Science/Math** | Atom-like crystal structures, floating geometric shapes, or energy orbs. |
| **Business/Finance** | Golden faceted gems, floating high-tech coins, or crystalline towers. |

---

## 🤖 Prompt Engineering Template

Use the following template for AI Image Generators (e.g., Midjourney, DALL-E 3):

> **Prompt:** `[Subject Metaphor] representing [Course Topic], 3D isometric game asset, high-gloss finish, floating in a dark cinematic void, volumetric lighting, neon [Accent Color] glows, ultra-detailed, 8k resolution, Unreal Engine 5 render style, --v 6.0`

---

## 🛠️ Technical Specifications

- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (Square) for Realm/Course icons.
- **Resolution:** Minimum 1024x1024px.
- **File Format:** PNG (with transparency where possible) or high-quality WebP.
- **Background:** Deep tinted dark (`#0b0c15`)—never pure `#000000`.

---

## 🚫 What to Avoid
- **Human Figures:** Avoid realistic human faces or figures unless it's a specific "Avatar" course.
- **Text in Images:** Do not include any text, titles, or watermarks inside the image.
- **Clutter:** No busy backgrounds or multiple subjects. Keep it iconic and clean.
